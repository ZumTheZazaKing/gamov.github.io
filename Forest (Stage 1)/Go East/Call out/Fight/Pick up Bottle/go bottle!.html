Without hesitation, you leap towards the bottle and pick it up from the ground. The bear
responds by charging at you with full speed. You don't really know what you can accomplish with
the bottle. However, you start to recognize what the powder in the bottle is. It was gunpowder.
Knowing how gunpowder works, you threw the bottle at the vicious bear and it exploded upon
impact.

BOOM!

The forest was shaken and your entire body was engulfed in smokes of carbon. After some time,
the smoke was cleared and you could see the remains of the bear you once fought lying on the
ground, burnt to a crisp. As things couldn't get any better, it did.

Whirr...

It was the sound of a helicopter. The pilot must have been drawn to the loud explosion and pillar of smoke
you made. As soon as the helicopter came into sight, you wave at it. The pilot could see you and he hovered the
helicopter above your location. With one simple push of a button, the pilot brought down a very long
rope, which you will then use to climb on to the helicopter and get a ride with the pilot.
