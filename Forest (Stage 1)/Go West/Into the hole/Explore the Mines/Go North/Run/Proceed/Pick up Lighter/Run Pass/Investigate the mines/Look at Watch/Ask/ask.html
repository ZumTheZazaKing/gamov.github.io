"Do any of you guys understand me?", you ask with the hopes of having at least one of them
capable of understanding human language.

After a few seconds of awkward silence, the red and green smoke finally responded by shaking
their heads. How did you know that they did so despite them not having heads? It was just a hunch.

However, it didn't take long for you to get a actual response.

"I can understand you very clearly, human", a voice resonated from behind.

You turn around and you see that black smoke had instantly arisen from the ground. White, green, red
and now black. The black smoke formed right in front of you and it showed no signs of fear or
caution of your ignited lighter.

"Who are you? What do you want?", the black smoke questions as he appears to be the leader of all
of the smoke that has appeared before you.

Whatever shall you say?

"I want to kill you!" "I want to ask some questions" "Umm..." "..."
